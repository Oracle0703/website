## Core objective

A check-in feature should optimize for long-term behavior quality, not just daily button clicks.

The design challenge is to balance:

- accessibility: low barrier for daily participation
- aspiration: meaningful long-term progression
- fairness: abuse-resistant scoring and transparent penalties

## Rule layers

A practical layering strategy:

1. daily baseline reward
2. streak milestone bonuses (3/7/14/30/etc.)
3. high-effort tasks with higher-value rewards
4. anti-abuse thresholds and cooldown windows

## Reward strategy

Use a dual-curve model:

- predictable daily rewards to maintain continuity
- milestone and deep-task rewards to encourage investment

This avoids both flat boredom and over-gamified spikes.

## Anti-abuse strategy

Anti-abuse should be explainable and reversible:

- trigger freeze before hard punishment
- define numeric thresholds in advance
- apply cooldowns to high-risk actions
- keep an audit trail for major adjustments

## Suggested metrics

Track these weekly:

- 7-day retention
- streak break rate
- deep-task completion rate
- anti-abuse trigger rate
- post-penalty recovery rate
